Coffee Book

An App to collect Recipe & write recipe, and look friend's favorite coffee

  • App main fuction and design

Elements

  • Writing interface

    To write new coffee recipe, user need to fill out Drink->whipping->syrup. When all three of them are same, then the system will consider the recipe as same as already existing one, so the user need to make different one.

  • Modal Task

    Pop-ups to give informations of same recipes are used, also full-screen size pop up is to be used to give the barcode of my friend's favorite coffe menu. So coffee shop worker just need to scan the barcode.

  • Tap bar

    Tap bar include three basic icons: Favorites, Home, Bookmarkd. Bookmark stands for recipe collecting & writing, Home is for "Best recipe of week," "Recommendations," "New recipes". and Favorite tab shows friends' and my favorite coffee by brands.
